AD: "Why does my life feel… stuck?"
T: "Because you stopped exploring."

AD: "Exploring what? I go out, I hang out…"
T: "Not places. Yourself."

AD: "What does that even mean?"
T: "Your brain is designed for uncertainty. New paths. New risks. That’s how humans evolved. But you wake up, same room, same road, same routine."

AD: "So routine is bad?"
T: "Routine is safe. But too much safety slowly kills curiosity."

AD: "So what’s the fix?"
T: "Go somewhere where you don’t know the language. Where Google Maps feels useless. Where you have to figure things out again."

AD: "That sounds uncomfortable."
T: "Exactly. That’s where you meet the version of you that isn’t stuck."
